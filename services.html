Our Funding Process

A streamlined approach to connecting startups with investors

1

Initial Application

Submit your comprehensive application form online, detailing your project, financial needs, and expected impact. Ensure all required documents are attached for a smooth review process.

2

Review and Due Diligence

Our team conducts a thorough review of your application, including financial analysis, project viability assessment, and background checks. We may contact you for additional information or clarification during this phase.

3

Approval and Offer

Upon successful review, we will issue a formal funding offer outlining the terms, conditions, and disbursement schedule. You will have the opportunity to review and accept the offer.

4

Funding Disbursement

Once the offer is accepted and all legal documentation is finalized, the approved funds will be disbursed according to the agreed-upon schedule, allowing you to commence or continue your project.
